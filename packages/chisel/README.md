## Design Principles

- The sculptor (human) and the rock (AI) are visual equals
- Sculptor elements: warm gold `#C9963A`, serif typography, organic shapes
- Rock elements: cool silver `#7B8DA6`, geometric patterns, luminous glow
- Meeting point: gradient blending both colors
- No generic AI aesthetics — this should feel like a craftsman's studio
